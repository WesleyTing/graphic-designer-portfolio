The best time to plant a tree was 10 years ago.
The second best time is now.

I am a graphic designer specializing in digital design, illustration and UX/UI design.

I picked up my first pencil at the age of 6, and ever since then, drawing has been my biggest hobby. Throughout my life, I have done numerous illustration comissions and community design projects, despite working in an unrelated field.

Then a year ago, I decided to take the leap and expand into designing banners and websites as a freelancer, in hopes of pursuing a career that I could be proud of and love.

I have always believed passion to be the strongest and most important driving force in a career, which is why I commited to my aspirations of becoming a graphic designer, so I can combine my work and interest into one.
