The 5 o’clock cafe is a casual cafe shop that provides a cozy and chill place for friends after work with multiple drink options.

My goal for 5 o’clock cafe branding deisgn focus on to express the happiness through sharing delicious food and drinks with friends after work. The tagline for the cafe shop is “Enjoy your Break” and the whole design project will adopt this idea and create the visual deliveries.

Statistics show that in Canada, 59% of under-35s believe a barista-prepared beverage adds value to their purchase, which is also the target group (25-35 year-old) for the 5 o’clock Cafe.

Bokara Grey and Linen White are the 2 main color pallete I choose for the cafe shop (simple, represent coffee color, clear contrast to make fun and quiet). Secondary color is Bali Hai Blue, which create friendly and chill environment.

The idea for 5 o’clock Cafe is a combination of coffee bean and a clock shape with hands pointing to 5 : 00. After dozens of initial skecth brainstorming, I finally adopt an illustrative outline and can be presented in both dark and light background.

The VI design, I extend to the most common cafe things, like cups (considering to-go during pandemic), menu & other prints, paper bags and stickers.
