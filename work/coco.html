CoCo is a shop with a large variety of beverages and food. Their goal is to showcase exotic drinks, and cater to the tastes of a wide audience.

CoCo asked for a series of posters to display their brand in their store and online. The posters focused on imagery of their products, to entice consumers to visit their shop.

The following posters are displayed throughout the store, and can be used as wallpaper, posters, menu suggestions, and prop-up banners.

My main considering throughout the process was color, and how I could make every drink match with one another. Since each drink was so different, I could come up with different design for each poster, which matched the exotic theme of the shop.
